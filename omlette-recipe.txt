Ingredients
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
- masala
- salt
- oil
- water

Directions:
1. Take egg yolk into a glass
2. Also take chilli powder, masala, salt into the glass.
3. Pour some water and mix it.
4. Heat a pan on stove for a minute and have oil on it.
5. Let the oil heat and pour the mixture on the pan.
6. Wait for it to cook so that you can reverse it.
7. If it cooked to that point then reverse it and let other side also cook.
8. If it is cooked omlette is ready.
